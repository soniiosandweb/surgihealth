Our Story

Revolutionizing Healthcare: Meet the Visionary Founder Behind SurgiHealthcare Solution

In 2014, Joshua Williams, while working as a sterile processing technician in Cincinnati, OH, founded SurgiHealth Solutions. Recognizing the urgent need for a lower-costing alternative supplier, Joshua was excited to bring efficiency and innovation to the sterile processing industry. SurgiHealth Solutions is committed to streamlining operations and providing hospitals with the best tools to get the job done. Our award-winning company fulfills the critical needs of healthcare facilities nationwide with cost-effective, high-quality reliable solutions, continuously pushing the boundaries of excellence in sterile processing.
